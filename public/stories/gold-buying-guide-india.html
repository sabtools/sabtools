24K vs 22K vs 18K

24K = 99.9% pure (coins, bars)

22K = 91.6% pure (jewellery)

18K = 75% pure (daily wear)

Always ask the exact purity before buying!

Making Charges: The Hidden Cost

8-25%

Jewellers charge 8-25% as making charges. That ₹50,000 necklace? ₹10,000 could be just making cost!

Always negotiate making charges

GST on Gold = 3%

3% GST on gold value + 5% GST on making charges. This is added to your final bill.

₹1 lakh gold = ₹3,000 GST + making GST

Check the bill breakdown!

Hallmark & HUID

BIS Hallmark is now mandatory. Every piece gets a unique 6-digit HUID number. Verify it on the BIS website!

No HUID? Don't buy!

Best Time to Buy Gold

Akshaya Tritiya and Dhanteras have festive discounts. But always check the live gold rate first!
