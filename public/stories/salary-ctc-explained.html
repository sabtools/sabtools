CTC vs In-Hand Salary

Know the Difference!

Every employee must know this →

What is CTC?

Cost to Company = Total amount the company spends on you. It includes everything — salary, PF, insurance, bonuses and perks.

CTC ≠ Your bank balance

CTC Components

Basic Salary (40-50%)

HRA (40-50% of Basic)

Special Allowance

Employer PF + Insurance + Bonus

Deductions That Eat Your Salary

Employee PF: 12% of Basic

Professional Tax: ₹200/month

Income Tax: As per slab

These are deducted every month before salary credit!

Real Example

CTC: ₹10 LPA

₹72,000

Approximate monthly in-hand

~28% goes to PF, tax, insurance and employer contributions!

Salary Negotiation Tips

Always ask for the in-hand breakup, not just CTC. Negotiate higher Basic for better PF and gratuity. Check if bonuses are guaranteed or variable.
