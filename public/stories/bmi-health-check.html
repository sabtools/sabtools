What is BMI?

Body Mass Index

Weight (kg) ÷ Height (m)²

A quick way to check if your weight is appropriate for your height.

BMI Categories

⬇️ Under 18.5 — Underweight

✅ 18.5 – 24.9 — Normal

⚠️ 25 – 29.9 — Overweight

🔴 30+ — Obese

🇮🇳 Indian BMI Standards

Different from WHO guidelines!

✅ Normal: 18.5 – 22.9

⚠️ Overweight: 23 – 24.9

🔴 Obese: 25+

Indians have higher body fat at lower BMI levels

Health Risks of High BMI

❤️ Heart disease & high BP

🩸 Type 2 Diabetes

🦴 Joint problems & arthritis

😴 Sleep apnea & breathing issues

Early detection saves lives!

Quick Tips to Improve BMI

🚶 Walk 10,000 steps daily

🥗 Eat more protein & fiber

💧 Drink 3+ litres of water

😴 Get 7-8 hours of sleep
