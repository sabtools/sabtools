4 GST Slabs in India

5% — Essential goods

12% — Standard goods

18% — Most services

28% — Luxury & sin goods

Common Items & Their GST

🍚 Packed food items — 5%

📱 Mobile phones — 12%

💻 IT services — 18%

🏠 Cement — 28%

🥛 Milk, fruits, vegetables — 0% (Exempt!)

How to Calculate GST

Adding GST:

Price + (Price × GST%) = Final Price

₹1,000 + 18% = ₹1,180

Removing GST:

Price ÷ (1 + GST%) = Base Price

₹1,180 ÷ 1.18 = ₹1,000

Input Tax Credit (ITC)

Pay GST on purchases? Claim it back!

ITC lets businesses deduct GST paid on inputs from GST collected on sales.

You only pay the difference to the government!
